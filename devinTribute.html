Devin Townsend

    With a musical career spanning twenty five years, and roughly as many albums to show for it, Devin Townsend has long been one of metal's biggest names. Devin released his first album in 1995, with a band named Strapping Young Lad. Since then, Devin has continued to release new and exciting music, sometimes even inventing new sub-genres of metal. His work ranges from hard rock, progressive metal, new-age, and ambient, if it can be classified as any one genre at all. He is known for his signature wall of sound, his down to earth personality, and his beautiful scalp. Creative freedom and expression has always been the focus of Townsend's passion with music, and his latest album, Empath, displays that greatly. Devin Townsend's music has been very important to me throughout my life, and I'm eager to see what he will do next; I hope you'll end up looking forward to it as well!
